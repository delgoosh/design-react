Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

This is a placeholder. The full Privacy Policy will be published before the public launch of Delgoosh.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, such as your name, email address, and session-related data necessary to deliver our services.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the Delgoosh platform.
  • To match patients with appropriate therapists.
  • To process payments and manage session tickets.
  • To communicate with you about your account and sessions.

3. Data Security

All therapy sessions are end-to-end encrypted. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal and health information.

4. Data Retention

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time through the in-app settings.

5. Third Parties

We do not sell your personal information. Data is shared only with your assigned therapist and essential service providers required to operate the platform.

6. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us through the in-app support system or review our Terms of Service.
